NANOscreen®

Discover the best formulation, fast.

Optimized Early-Stage Research and Screening

NANOscreen® is a user-friendly system designed for rapid formulation screening and identification of an early-stage product formulation with minimal material losses. The NANOscreen®, augmented by the FR-JET® mixing technology, in combination with our intuitive user interface, enables precise control over all relevant process parameters.

Key Benefits

Rapid Screening

Rapid screening and formulation identification with minimal material consumption.

Direct Transfer

Methods directly transferable to NANOlab — no redevelopment required.

Minimal Volumes

Batch sizes as small as 0.5 mL with flow rates up to 100 mL/min.

Minimal Losses

Engineered to minimize material losses during the screening process.
