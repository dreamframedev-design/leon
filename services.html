Capabilities

Process Development & Optimisation

Our experienced team of scientists can support you with your process development needs using the FR-JET® Technology and LEON systems.

Nanoencapsulation

Nanoparticle-based drug delivery systems used as carrier vehicles for protection and targeted delivery of drug substances — genetic material, RNA, small molecules. Our FR-JET® Technology offers complete flexibility in the nanocarrier material: lipid-based (LNPs, liposomes), polymeric (PLGA), or inorganic.

Nanoprecipitation

Nanoprecipitation with the FR-JET® Technology generates homogeneous batches of amorphous or crystalline nanoparticulate small molecules under continuous manufacturing. A powerful solution to expedite development of APIs, revitalize forgotten formulations, and deliver improved stability, increased solubility, and enhanced bioavailability.
