NANOlab®

Benchtop system for process development and non-clinical studies.

Start with the right technology and scale with confidence

Seamlessly transition your initial formulation from NANOscreen to NANOlab®. The NANOlab delivers batch sizes as small as 1 mL all the way through to continuous operation. With flow rates ranging from 6-500 mL/min, enabled through precise control of our patented FR-JET mixing technology, hitting your target nanoparticle specifications for every batch is easy. The process parameters identified using our NANOlab system can be directly scaled to our GMP-ready NANOme or NANOus systems, without the need for intermediate steps or additional equipment. Supports all non-clinical development studies.
