FR-JET® Technology

A patented modular mixing platform that produces identical nanoparticle quality from first screening to full GMP scale.

How FR-JET® Works

The FR-JET® mixer uses a free-jet impingement principle — two liquid streams are focused through precision-engineered nozzles and collide at supersonic speeds in a mixing chamber. The result is ultrafast, turbulent mixing that produces highly uniform nanoparticles with narrow size distributions.

Unlike conventional mixing technologies, the FR-JET® mixer is fully modular: the same mixing head is used across all four LEON systems (NANOscreen, NANOlab, NANOme, NANOus), ensuring that formulations developed on the benchtop scale identically at large-volume GMP production.
