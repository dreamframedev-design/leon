OUR SYSTEMS

One mixer. Four systems. Benchtop to GMP.

Leon-nanodrugs nanoparticle manufacturing portfolio is built on a single core technology: Our patented FR-JET® mixer, the industry's first precisely engineered mixer designed for seamless transition from R&D to GMP. No re-development, no process re-engineering, no dead ends.

  • NANOscreen® for rapid screening and formulation identification
  • NANOlab® for process development non-GMP manufacturing
  • NANOme® for pump-free, cassette-based GMP batch manufacturing
  • NANOus® for large-scale continuous production
